Cinnamon Rolls

Home

This tasty cinnamon roll recipe is perfect for anyone with a sweet tooth. Not only will this recipe make a warm, sweet breakfast treat, but they are buttery and will melt in your mouth with the most amazing flavor.

Ingredients

Dough

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 packages active dry yeast
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 6 cups flour

Filling

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 4 teaspoons cinnamon

Icing

  • 2 cups powdered sugar
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 4 tablespoons milk
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract

Directions

Making the Dough

  1. Dissolve the active dry yeast in warm water.
  2. Once dissolved, add eggs, butter, salt, sugar, and 3 cups of flour to the mixture.
  3. Mix and knead until a ball is formed.
  4. Gradually add the rest of the flour until the dough is slightly sticky.
  5. Place the dough in the refrigerator covered overnight.

Making the Rolls

  1. Mix the brown sugar and cinnamon together.
  2. Roll out the dough and spread the butter evenly on top.
  3. Add the brown sugar and cinnamon on top of the butter.
  4. Roll the dough and cut into individual rolls.
  5. Grease a pan and cover the cinnamon rolls, letting them rise to double their size.
  6. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees.
  7. After an hour, put the cinnamon rollls in the oven for about 20 to 25 minutes.

Making the Icing

  1. Mix powdered sugar, melted butter, milk, and vanilla extract in a bowl.
  2. Once smooth, pour over the warm cinnamon rolls and enjoy!
