Chocolate Chip Cookies

Home

If you're looking for a simple sweet treat, these delicious chocolate chip cookies will do the trick!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup salted butter
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 1/4 cup flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Preheat the over to 375 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Cream the butter and sugars together.
  3. Add the eggs one at a time to the mixture.
  4. Add the vanilla, flour, baking soda, and salt.
  5. Once mixed well, stir in the chocolate chips.
  6. Roll the mixture into the size cookie of your choice.
  7. Bake at 375 degrees F for 7 to 9 minutes.
  8. Let the cookies cool and enjoy!
