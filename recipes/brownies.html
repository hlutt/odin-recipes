Fudge Brownies

Home

If you're in the mood for a delicious chocolatey treat, give this simple brownie recipe a try.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla
  • 2/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Grease a 9x13 baking pan.
  3. Melt the butter and stir in sugar, eggs, and vanilla.
  4. Stir in cocoa powder, flour, salt, and baking powder.
  5. Add in the chocolate chips and pour the batter into the baking dish.
  6. Bake for 25 to 30 minutes and enjoy!
